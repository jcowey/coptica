# Rules for processing

- Replace `\d{3}` with `P.Mon.Epiph. \1`
- Replace `\n(\d{2})\n` with `\n\1. `
- Replace `-\n(\d{2})\n` with `\n\1.-  `
- Replace `⳨` with `*rho-cross*`
- Replace `+` with `*stauros*`
- Replace `?` with `/*?*/`
- Replace `·` with `*middot*`
- Replace `⳿` with `*word-sep-apostrophe*`
